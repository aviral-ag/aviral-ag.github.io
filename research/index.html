Publications

  • Financial viability assessment of concentrated solar power technologies under Indian climatic conditions.
    2020
    Sustainable Energy Technologies and Assessments
    Sankalp Kumar, Aviral Agarwal, Dr. Anil Kumar

    The study identifies Jodhpur as the optimal location for a 50 MW Solar Power Tower CSP plant in India, with the lowest LCOE and highest NPV, aiding in technology and investment decisions.

  • Comparative study on air quality status in Indian and Chinese cities before and during the COVID-19 lockdown period. Air Quality
    2020
    Atmosphere and Health, 13(10), 1167–1178
    Aviral Agarwal, Aman Kaushik, Sankalp Kumar, Dr. Rajeev Kumar Mishra

    Lockdown measures during COVID-19 significantly reduced AQI levels of PM2.5 and NO2 in major cities of India and China, highlighting effective pathways for air pollution mitigation.

  • Predicting Ground Level PM2.5 Concentration Over Delhi Using Landsat 8 Satellite Data
    2020
    International Journal of Remote Sensing, 42(3), 827-838
    Dr. Rajeev Kumar Mishra, Aviral Agarwal, Ankita Shukla

    A multispectral empirical model using Landsat 8 data effectively estimates PM2.5 concentrations in Delhi with adequate accuracy, correlating satellite reflectance with in-situ measurements and meteorological data.
