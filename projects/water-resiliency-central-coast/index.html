Water Resiliency Model for Central Coast California

September 2021 - February 2023

Climate and GIS-based water infrastructure vulnerability and resilience indexing for the Central Coast.

Hydrology

Overview

Analyzed climate change impacts on Central California water infrastructure using GIS and DWR data to assess basin vulnerabilities. Provided data-driven insights that supported five regional water management plans.

Resilience index

Led a team of three in developing a Python script computing a dynamic resilience index for San Luis Obispo County, integrating 10+ data sources on water demand, supply, and climate projections to enhance drought preparedness efforts.
