## Overview

Executed a data-driven flood analysis using concepts of expected loss and exceedance probability along with 10,000 Monte Carlo simulations.

## Outcomes

Proposed a hybrid solution combining insurance and levees, reducing potential annual losses by 40% and eliminating risk of $400K+ damages.
