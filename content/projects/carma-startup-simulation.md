## Overview

Spearheaded a six-month startup incubation project with a team of five to develop a carbon management app, overseeing program management, team onboarding, milestone tracking, and resource allocation while implementing incentives through discounts and gamification.

## Outreach

Presented the concept at TreeHacks 2023 and the Stanford Graduate School of Business Ignite Program in 2023.
