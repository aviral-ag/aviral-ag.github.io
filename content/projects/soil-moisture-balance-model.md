## Overview

Developed an R-based soil moisture balance model to simulate daily crop water use and accurately estimate irrigation demand across a 100,000+ acre agricultural basin in California. The model integrated climate, crop, soil, and land use data to dynamically track soil moisture and drive water demand calculations.

## Groundwater and MODFLOW

To evaluate groundwater sustainability, I executed MODFLOW 6 simulations using FloPy, modeling various pumping scenarios to assess spatial and temporal stress on the aquifer system. This analysis supported decision-making for one of California’s most over-drafted groundwater basins, providing key insights for sustainable water management strategies under SGMA, including conjunctive use, managed aquifer recharge, and demand reduction planning.
